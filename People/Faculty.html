Introduction

A.P Zhang Weizhong graduated from Harbin Engineering University with a bachelor’s degree in 2002, a master’s degree from Shanghai Jiaotong University in 2005, and a doctorate degree from the University of Louisville in 2009. He has been engaged in teaching and research at Beijing Institute of Technology since 2010. The main courses include “Computer Control Principles” and “Adaptive and Robust Control” for graduate students. The work is down-to-earth, earnest and continuous improvement, and has won awards such as outstanding Communist Party members of Beijing Institute of Technology, the second prize of the 16th Excellent Education and Teaching Achievement Award, school-level outstanding class teacher, and outstanding master’s instructor. He has compiled the textbook “Adaptive Control Theory and Application”, the monograph “Optimal Trajectory Realization Technology in Discrete Mechanics”, and the translated book “Aircraft Control Distribution”. He has published more than 20 papers in important journals and conferences at home and abroad, and has been cited more than 150 times by personal Google Scholar. Served as a member of the Robot Competition Professional Committee of the Chinese Society of Automation.
